# KODA — Guerrilla Marketing Tactics

## 15 Unconventional Ideas (Ranked by ROI)

### Tier 1: Do These First 🔥

**1. Competitor Death Watch**
- Set Google Alerts for "Notion down", "Evernote outage", "Obsidian sync error"
- When they go down, immediately post on the Reddit/Twitter thread: *"This is why local-first matters. [KODA link]"*
- Cost: $0 | Impact: High — catches users when they're most angry

**2. SaaS Ransom Calculator**
- Web tool: input monthly subscriptions → calculate 10-year "ransom"
- "You'll spend $12,000 on notes over your lifetime. KODA is $15 once."
- Post results on r/personalfinance, Twitter
- Cost: $0 | Impact: High — loss aversion = conversions

**3. Notion Migration Script**
- Open-source Python script: exports Notion databases → clean .md files in iCloud
- Removes #1 switching friction
- Post on GitHub, r/selfhosted, HN
- Cost: $0 | Impact: High — you become the "liberator"

**4. Subscription Burn Receipt**
- User tweets screenshot canceling Notion/Evernote → gets KODA 50% off
- Turns customers into competitor assassins
- Cost: $7.50/sale | Impact: High — viral loop

**5. End of Life Guarantee**
- Legal ToS commitment: "If I abandon this app, I open-source the entire codebase"
- Solves #1 objection for note-takers: "what if you disappear?"
- Cost: $0 | Impact: High — trust builder

### Tier 2: Do These During Launch

**6. Product Hunt "Anti-Launch"**
- Title: "The App That Doesn't Want to Keep You"
- Focus on the fact users can leave anytime because files stay on their drive
- Cost: $0 | Impact: High

**7. App Store SEO Hijack**
- Keywords: "alternative to Notion", "no subscription", "markdown offline"
- Description includes comparison table: KODA $15 once vs Notion $120/yr
- Cost: $0 | Impact: Medium-High

**8. Digital Minimalism YouTube Circuit**
- Pitch Matt D'Avella-type creators, not tech reviewers
- They care about philosophy of ownership, not tech specs
- Offer free Pro + affiliate revenue share
- Cost: $0 | Impact: Medium-High

**9. Mastodon/fosstodon.org Tribe**
- The federated crowd is allergic to big tech
- Build presence over weeks, share dev journey
- Cost: $0 | Impact: Medium

**10. "One-Time Payment" Badge**
- Create a badge/sticker: "Proudly Subscription-Free"
- Distribute to other indie devs. Build a directory of "Pay Once" apps
- Cost: $0 | Impact: Medium

### Tier 3: Creative / Unconventional

**11. Markdown ASMR (TikTok/Reels)**
- High-quality close-up video: typing in markdown on iPad, mechanical keyboard sounds
- No talking. Just visual + audio of plain text files appearing in a folder
- Appeals to "Digital Minimalist" aesthetic
- Cost: $0 | Impact: Medium

**12. "Inheritance" Feature (Legacy Mode)**
- Generates a PDF: "How to access my .md files without KODA"
- For next-of-kin. Reinforces "own forever" positioning viscerally
- Cost: $0 | Impact: Medium

**13. Anti-VC Manifesto**
- "Why I turned down millions" — VCs kill note apps by forcing AI/bloat
- Build tribe of users supporting indie craftsman against "The Machine"
- Cost: $0 | Impact: Medium

**14. University "Digital Sovereignty" Workshops**
- Free guest lectures at NUS/NTU/SMU: "Why your thesis shouldn't live in a database you don't own"
- Get 100+ students in a room for an hour as the "expert"
- Cost: $0 (travel time) | Impact: Medium

**15. #MarkdownArt Challenge**
- Start a trend on X/Mastodon showing how beautiful plain text can look
- Cross-pollinate with designer/developer communities
- Cost: $0 | Impact: Low-Medium
