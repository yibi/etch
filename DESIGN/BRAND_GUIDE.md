# KODA — Brand Guide

## Brand Identity

**KODA** — Short, punchy, global. Sounds like "code." Japanese for "echo." Works in English, Japanese, German, any language. Four letters, two syllables, easy to say, easy to type, easy to remember.

### Brand Promise
*"Your files, your cloud, your rules. Pay once, own forever."*

### Brand Personality
- **Not** corporate, enterprise, or "SaaS"
- **Not** warm, friendly, or "helpful assistant"
- **Is:** confident, direct, slightly rebellious, pro-tool
- **Vibe:** If Notion is a friendly office manager, KODA is the senior engineer who tells you the truth

### Tone of Voice
- Direct. No fluff. No "Hey there! 👋"
- Technical but not jargon-heavy
- Anti-subscription without being angry about it
- Confident, not arrogant
- "Your notes are hostages. KODA is the exit." — this is the attitude

### Target Audience

| Audience | Role | What they want |
|----------|------|----------------|
| Gen Z (15-25) | Hype engine | Aesthetic, speed, ownership, anti-corporate |
| Millennials (28-43) | Revenue engine | Privacy, no subscriptions, stability, data ownership |

### Visual Direction

**Not:** Glassmorphism, gradients, emoji, cute illustrations, "consumer app" look
**Is:** Bold typography, high contrast, monospace accents, dark-mode native, "tool" not "toy"

**References:** Arc browser, Linear, Raycast, Things 3, Bear — clean, confident, pro

*Note: Accent color should be bold and singular. One accent, not five.*

### Typography

| Use | Font | Weight |
|-----|------|--------|
| Display/headlines | SF Pro Display | Bold |
| Body | SF Pro Text | Regular |
| Code/markdown | SF Mono or JetBrains Mono | Regular |
| App icon text | Custom or SF Pro Rounded | Black |

### App Icon Direction
- Minimal. 1-2 colors max.
- Not: letter "K", notebook icon, pencil icon
- Consider: abstract mark, geometric shape, "tool" aesthetic
- Must work at 60x60 (widget) and 1024x1024 (App Store)
- No gradient. No skeuomorphism. Flat or subtle depth.

### Naming Convention

```
KODA: Notes      ← First app
KODA: Ledger    ← Second app (health/habits)
KODA: Tally      ← Third app (finance/inventory)
```

Colon separator. Consent: "KODA is the brand, the word after colon is the function."

### Competitor Positioning

| Competitor | Their weakness | KODA's strength |
|------------|----------------|-----------------|
| Notion | $10/mo, cloud-only, slow, bloated | $15 once, offline, fast, 10MB |
| Obsidian | $8/mo for sync, complex, desktop-first | Free sync (iOS native), simple, mobile-first |
| Apple Notes | Locked to Apple, proprietary format | Open `.md` files, cross-platform future |
| Bear | iOS only, $2.99/mo, proprietary format | One-time, plain files, cross-platform future |
| Evernote | $15/mo, bloated, dying | $15 once, minimal, alive |
